Everything you need

From day one to pro level — ÍslandFit goes the distance with you.

AI Coach

A personal AI coach that remembers you: weekly check-ins, answers to your questions, and program adjustments based on your progress.

Sport-specific programs

Science-based programs for every sport — strength, power, speed and endurance, tailored to your goal and equipment.

Progress & PRs

Track every lift, see personal records and charts of your progress. Watch the numbers prove you're getting stronger.

Human coaches

Connect with a certified coach who sees your progress, assigns programs and builds custom plans — right inside the app.
